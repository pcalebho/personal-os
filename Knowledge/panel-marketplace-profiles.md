## Platform-Specific Setup

### 1. MFG.com (Priority: HIGH)

**URL:** https://www.mfg.com/

**Steps:**
1. Go to mfg.com → Click "Suppliers" or "Join as Supplier"
2. Create account with business email
3. Complete company profile using content above
4. Select capabilities:
   - Fabrication → Electrical Assembly
   - Fabrication → Panel Wiring
   - Fabrication → Control Systems
5. Add certifications (UL 508A)
6. Set location (Austin, TX) and shipping radius (nationwide)
7. Upload photos of completed panels (if available)
8. Start browsing and quoting RFQs

**Live RFQs:** https://www.mfg.com/live-rfqs/

---

### 2. ThomasNet (Priority: HIGH)

**URL:** https://www.thomasnet.com/

**Steps:**
1. Go to thomasnet.com → Click "List Your Company" or "Add Your Company"
2. Create free supplier profile
3. Select categories:
   - Electric Control Panels
   - Custom Control Panels
   - Industrial Control Panels
   - Motor Control Panels

4. Fill in company details using content above
5. Add certifications, capabilities, industries served

**Your listing will appear when buyers search for control panels in Texas.**

---

### 3. SAM.gov (Priority: MEDIUM - Government work)

**URL:** https://sam.gov/

**Steps:**
1. Go to sam.gov → Click "Sign In" → Create Login.gov account
2. Complete Entity Registration (requires EIN/Tax ID)
3. Get your CAGE code (assigned during registration)
4. Select NAICS codes:
   - 335313 - Switchgear and Switchboard Apparatus Manufacturing
   - 335314 - Relay and Industrial Control Manufacturing
   - 334419 - Other Electronic Component Manufacturing
5. Complete representations and certifications
6. Set up "Saved Searches" for:
   - "control panel"
   - "electrical panel"
   - "switchgear"
   - "industrial controls"
7. Enable email alerts for matching opportunities

**Note:** Registration takes 7-10 business days to process.

---
