## Targeting Strategy

### Top Priorities for Conversations/Tours
1. **Direct manufacturing OEMs** - Companies building equipment that needs control panels:
   - Semiconductor equipment manufacturers
   - Food processing equipment makers
   - Packaging machinery companies
   - Water/wastewater equipment manufacturers

2. **System Integrators** - Focus on those who:
   - Don't build their own panels in-house
   - Have overflow requirements
   - Need specialized UL compliance
   - Work on time-sensitive projects

3. **Panel Shops** - Established shops for:
   - Partnership opportunities
   - Overflow work
   - Knowledge/best practices exchange
   - Complementary capabilities

### Outreach Approach
1. **Geographic targeting:** Start with Austin, then expand to Houston, Dallas, San Antonio
2. **LinkedIn + email sequence** for initial contact
3. **Request site tours** to understand their operations
4. **Value proposition:** Fast turnaround, UL certified, local Austin-based
